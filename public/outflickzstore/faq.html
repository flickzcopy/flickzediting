Orders & Payments

How can I check my order status?

Once your order ships, we will email you an order notification. You can also log into your User Dashboard and view your **Order History** for real-time updates. Pre-order items will have delayed period to the available dates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major debit cards, including **Visa, Verve, and Mastercard**, processed securely through **Paystack**. We also accept direct **Bank Transfers**. All transactions are securely processed.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes **3-5 business days** after processing (1-2 days). For limited drops and international orders, times may vary. Check our Shipping & Returns page for detailed estimates.

What are "Pre-Order" items?

Pre-Order items are products that are not yet in stock but can be reserved in advance. The estimated delivery date is clearly stated on the product page. Purchasing a pre-order item guarantees you one of the limited edition pieces!

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within **7 days** of delivery for unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. Final sale items (e.g., clearance, limited drops) may be excluded. Please visit our Shipping & Returns page to initiate a return.

Where can I find a size guide?

Our unique streetwear pieces often feature an oversized fit. We recommend consulting the specific **Size Guide** on our Size Guide page for accurate measurements.
