Our pieces are designed for a relaxed, authentic streetwear fit. Use these charts to find your perfect size. All measurements are in **Inches (in)**.

Quick Sizing Tip

**Outflickz Wear is designed with an Oversized/Boxy Fit.** If you prefer a more standard or fitted look, consider sizing down. Always measure your favorite fitting garment and compare the measurements to the table below.

Wears (Tops, Hoodies, Jackets)

Measure across the chest from armpit to armpit (Chest Width) and from the top of the shoulder to the bottom hem (Length).

Size Chest Width (in) Length (in) Suggested Fit
S 22 28 Relaxed
M 23 29 Relaxed / Oversized
L 24 30 Oversized
XL 25 31 Very Oversized
2XL 26 32 Max Oversized

Trousers & Bottoms

Measure your waist where you naturally wear your trousers. The length listed below is the Inseam (crotch to ankle hem).

Size (Inches) Waist (Relaxed/Stretched) Inseam Length Equivalent
S (28-30) 28 / 30 30 Small
M (30-32) 30 / 32 31 Medium
L (32-34) 32 / 34 32 Large
XL (34-36) 34 / 36 32 X-Large

Cap Collections

All Outflickz caps are designed as **One-Size-Fits-Most (OSFM)** with an adjustable closure (snapback, strapback, or buckle) for a customized, comfortable fit.

Head Circumference Guide

  • **Standard Fit:** Suitable for head circumference between **21.5 inches (54.6 cm)** and **23.5 inches (59.7 cm)**.
  • **How to Measure:** Use a soft tape measure and wrap it around the fullest part of your head, just above your ears.
