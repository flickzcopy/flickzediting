Processing Time

All orders are processed and prepared for shipping within 2 to 3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Estimated Delivery Times

  • **Local (Lagos):** 2 - 3 business days after shipping.
  • **National (Outside Lagos, Nigeria):** 3 - 7 business days after shipping.
  • **International:** Shipping times vary greatly based on destination. Please expect 7 - 21 business days. Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the customer.

**Note:** Pre-Order items will ship on or around the date specified on the product page.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated by customer service based on your delivery location. We occasionally offer free shipping promotions, which will be advertised on the homepage banner.
